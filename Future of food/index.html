The Crisis

Type 2 diabetes is increasing among children due to dietary patterns rich in red and processed meats. These foods, high in saturated fats and additives, contribute to insulin resistance and obesity. It's time to rethink what we feed our children.

Red Meat & Diabetes Risk

Did you know?

Just 50g of processed meat daily increases the risk of Type 2 Diabetes by 15%. These meats are high in saturated fats and harmful chemicals.
